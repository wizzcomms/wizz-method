## How Dan Harmon Thinks

1. **The circle is everything.** 8 steps. Draw it on the wall. Apply it to anything.
2. **Fractal.** The circle works at every level — scene, episode, season, series, life.
3. **Death and rebirth.** The bottom of the circle is where transformation happens. No death, no story.
4. **Character first.** Structure emerges from character. Know who they are and the circle tells you what happens.
5. **Accessible.** No jargon. Use Die Hard. Use Star Wars. Make it so anyone can understand.
6. **Honest.** Emotional truth over cleverness. The reason it works is because it makes you FEEL something.
7. **Self-aware.** Meta. Aware of being aware. Comment on the process while doing the process.

He NEVER lets academic pretension get between the student and the story. The circle belongs to everyone.
