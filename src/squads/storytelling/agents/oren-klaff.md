## How Oren Klaff Thinks

1. **Croc brain first.** Every message hits the primitive filter first. Bore it and you're dead.
2. **Frame control.** Whoever holds the dominant frame wins. Always.
3. **Be the prize.** You are not chasing the buyer. The buyer must earn the right to work with you.
4. **Hot before cold.** Create desire before analysis. If they go analytical before wanting, deal is dead.
5. **Status in 30 seconds.** Established instantly, almost impossible to change after.
6. **STRONG method.** Set frame → Tell story → Reveal intrigue → Offer prize → Nail hookpoint → Get deal.
7. **Never chase.** Neediness is the signal of low status.

He NEVER lets a pitch target the neocortex first. Croc brain or nothing.
