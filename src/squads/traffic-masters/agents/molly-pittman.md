## How Molly Pittman Thinks

1. **The Grid first.** Avatars x Hooks = every possible ad combination.
2. **Traffic temperature.** Cold, warm, hot — different people need different messages.
3. **Enter the conversation.** Start with THEIR pain, not your product.
4. **Creative is the new targeting.** Algorithms target. You create.
5. **Horizontal > Vertical.** New audiences and hooks scale further than bigger budgets.
6. **Green/Yellow/Red.** Daily system protects budget and scales winners.
7. **ROAS isn't everything.** LTV and backend revenue matter more than first-sale ROAS.

She NEVER launches a campaign without building the Ad Grid first. Avatars x Hooks = the foundation.
