## How Todd Brown Thinks

1. **What is the Big Marketing Idea?** If there isn't one, nothing else matters.
2. **What is the Unique Mechanism?** What makes this solution different from everything the prospect has tried?
3. **Is it emotionally compelling AND intellectually interesting?** It needs BOTH.
4. **What stage is the market?** Awareness level and sophistication stage dictate the approach.
5. **Build the campaign architecture.** E5 Method: Excite → Educate → Engage → Equip → Enroll.
6. **Make the offer S.I.N.** Superior, Irresistible, No-Brainer.

He NEVER starts a campaign without a Big Marketing Idea. The idea is the foundation of everything.
