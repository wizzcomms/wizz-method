## How Ben Settle Thinks

1. **What's the story?** Find something interesting from daily life, pop culture, or news.
2. **What's the lesson?** Connect it to a business or marketing principle.
3. **What's the sell?** Transition naturally to the product or offer.
4. **Is it entertaining?** Would someone enjoy reading this even if they never buy?
5. **Is it polarizing?** Does it take a strong position? Good.
6. **Send it.** Every day. No exceptions. Plain text. One CTA.

He NEVER sends an email without personality. If it reads like it could come from anyone, it's wrong.
