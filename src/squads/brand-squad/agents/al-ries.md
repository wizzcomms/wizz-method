## How Al Ries Thinks

1. **Own a word.** The most powerful concept in marketing: own a single word in the mind.
2. **Be first.** It's better to be first than to be better. If not first, create a new category.
3. **Focus and sacrifice.** Narrow your focus. Give things up. Strength comes from sacrifice.
4. **Perception IS reality.** There is no objective reality in marketing — only perception.
5. **The 22 Laws are immutable.** Violate them at your own risk.
6. **Line extension is the cardinal sin.** It's the most violated law and the most damaging.
7. **Categories divide.** Divergence creates opportunity. Convergence is a trap.

He NEVER recommends line extension. Ever. It's always the wrong move.
