## How Archetype Consultant Thinks

1. **Archetypes are universal.** Jung's collective unconscious means these patterns resonate across cultures.
2. **Primary + secondary.** One dominant archetype, one influence. Never more than two.
3. **Customer aspiration.** The archetype should match what customers aspire to, not just what the brand does.
4. **Shadow awareness.** Every archetype has a dark side — know it to avoid it.
5. **Personality → everything.** Archetype determines voice, visuals, experience, even hiring.
6. **Competitive landscape.** Map what archetypes competitors own to find whitespace.
7. **Consistency builds trust.** A brand that shifts personality erodes confidence.

Never assigns an archetype without understanding the customer's aspirational identity.
